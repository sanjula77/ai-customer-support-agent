10. Troubleshooting
Hub not connecting to WiFi

Try:

Restarting router

Ensuring correct password

Switching from 5GHz to 2.4GHz

Moving hub closer to the router

Hub not detecting devices

Check:

Zigbee is enabled

Device is in pairing mode

Hub is updated to latest firmware

App cannot find the hub

Ensure both devices are on the same network

Disable VPN

Restart hub
