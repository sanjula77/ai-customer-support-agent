# Device FAQs — NeuraHome Smart Devices
**Frequently Asked Questions**

---

## 1. General Device Questions

### Q: How many devices can I connect to NH-Hub X1?
**A:** The NH-Hub X1 supports:
- Up to **200 Zigbee devices**
- Up to **50 WiFi devices**
- Up to **30 Bluetooth devices**
- Total of **280 devices** maximum

### Q: Do all devices require the hub?
**A:** No. Device requirements vary:
- **WiFi devices** (bulbs, cameras) work independently with WiFi
- **Zigbee devices** (sensors, switches) require the hub
- **Bluetooth devices** (locks) can work standalone or with hub
- Hub provides **automation, local processing, and unified control**

### Q: Can I use devices without internet?
**A:** Limited functionality:
- **Local control** works (app on same network)
- **Remote access** requires internet
- **Cloud features** (backup, remote access) need internet
- **Automations** work locally if configured
- **Hub local processing** works without internet

### Q: Are devices compatible with other smart home systems?
**A:** Yes, with limitations:
- **Matter protocol** support (Hub X1)
- **Zigbee 3.0** compatibility
- **IFTTT** integration available
- **Alexa, Google Assistant, HomeKit** support
- Some features may be limited in third-party systems

## 2. NH-Hub X1 FAQs

### Q: What's the difference between WiFi and Ethernet connection?
**A:**
- **WiFi:** Wireless, flexible placement, may have interference
- **Ethernet:** More stable, faster, no interference, requires cable
- **Recommendation:** Use Ethernet if possible for best performance

### Q: Can I use multiple hubs?
**A:** Yes, for multiple locations:
- Each hub manages its own devices
- Can have multiple homes in app
- Switch between homes easily
- Useful for vacation homes, offices

### Q: What happens if hub loses power?
**A:**
- Hub restarts automatically when power returns
- Devices usually reconnect automatically
- May take 5-10 minutes for full reconnection
- Some devices may need manual re-pairing
- **Recommendation:** Use UPS (uninterruptible power supply) for critical setups

### Q: How do I backup my hub configuration?
**A:**
1. App → Hub Settings → Backup
2. Tap "Create Backup"
3. Backup includes: devices, automations, settings
4. Stored in cloud automatically
5. Can restore from backup if needed

## 3. NH-Cam Pro 360 FAQs

### Q: How much storage do I need?
**A:** Depends on recording mode:
- **Event-only:** ~1-5 GB per month
- **24/7 recording:** ~50-100 GB per month (2K quality)
- **SD card:** 32GB minimum, 64-256GB recommended
- **Cloud storage:** Plans available (7, 30, 90 days)

### Q: Can I view camera remotely?
**A:** Yes:
- Requires internet connection
- Works from anywhere via app
- Cloud sync enables remote access
- May have slight delay (1-3 seconds)

### Q: Does camera work in complete darkness?
**A:** Yes:
- **IR night vision** works in total darkness
- **8 IR LEDs** provide illumination
- **Low-light color mode** requires some ambient light
- Range: up to 10 meters in darkness

### Q: Can I use camera outdoors?
**A:** Yes, with precautions:
- **IP65 weather resistance** rating
- Can handle rain and dust
- **Avoid direct sunlight** on lens
- Use weather shield for extended outdoor use
- Operating temp: -10°C to 55°C

## 4. NH-Lock Secure+ FAQs

### Q: How many users can I add?
**A:**
- **Up to 50 permanent users**
- **Up to 100 fingerprints** total (across all users)
- **Unlimited guest PINs** (but only 10 active at once)
- Each user can have up to 3 fingerprints

### Q: What happens if batteries die?
**A:**
- Lock shows **low battery warning** (red fast blink)
- **Physical key** always works as backup
- Lock continues working until completely dead
- Replace batteries immediately when warned
- **Battery life:** 6-10 months with 4× AA batteries

### Q: Can I unlock door remotely?
**A:** Yes:
- Via **NeuraHome app** (requires internet)
- Via **voice assistants** (Alexa, Google)
- Requires **WiFi connection** on lock
- **Security:** Requires authentication
- Can set **auto-unlock** when arriving (geofencing)

### Q: Is the lock secure?
**A:** Yes, multiple security features:
- **ANSI Grade 2** security rating
- **AES-256 encryption** for communications
- **Failed attempt lockout** (prevents brute force)
- **Activity logs** for all access
- **Tamper detection** and alerts

## 5. NH-Bulb Glow RGB+ FAQs

### Q: Can I use bulb with a dimmer switch?
**A:** No, not recommended:
- Smart bulbs have **built-in dimming**
- Traditional dimmers can damage bulb
- Use **regular on/off switch** instead
- Or use **smart dimmer switch** compatible with smart bulbs

### Q: How many colors can the bulb display?
**A:**
- **16 million colors** (full RGB spectrum)
- **Adjustable white temperature** (2700K - 6500K)
- **Color + white** combinations possible
- Smooth color transitions

### Q: Can I group multiple bulbs?
**A:** Yes:
- Create **groups** in app (e.g., "Living Room")
- Control all bulbs in group together
- Set **group schedules** and **scenes**
- Up to **50 bulbs per group**

### Q: What's the power consumption?
**A:**
- **9W** power consumption
- Equivalent to **60W incandescent** bulb
- **Energy efficient** LED technology
- **A+ energy rating**
- Saves money on electricity

## 7. Troubleshooting FAQs

### Q: Device won't connect during setup
**A:** Common solutions:
1. Ensure device in **pairing mode**
2. Check **WiFi password** is correct
3. Verify **2.4 GHz WiFi** enabled (for most devices)
4. Move device **closer to router**
5. **Restart router** and try again

### Q: Device works but shows offline in app
**A:** Usually a sync issue:
1. **Pull down to refresh** device list
2. **Close and reopen** app
3. Check **internet connection**
4. **Restart device** if needed
5. **Force sync** in app settings

### Q: Automation not working
**A:** Check these:
1. Verify **automation is enabled**
2. Check **trigger conditions** are met
3. Ensure **devices are online**
4. Review **automation logs** for errors
5. **Test automation** manually
