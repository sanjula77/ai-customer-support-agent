# NeuraHome Systems — Return Policy

## 1. Overview
NeuraHome Systems strives to ensure customer satisfaction.  
Our **Return Policy** allows customers to return eligible products within a specified timeframe for exchange or refund.

---

## 2. Eligibility
- Products must be purchased from **official NeuraHome stores or website**.  
- Returns accepted **within 30 days** of delivery.  
- Products must be **unused, undamaged, and in original packaging**.  
- Electronic accessories (cables, adapters) must be unopened.

---

## 3. Return Process
1. Log in to your NeuraHome account.  
2. Go to **Orders → Select Product → Request Return**.  
3. Provide reason for return.  
4. Receive **RMA (Return Merchandise Authorization) number**.  
5. Ship the product back to NeuraHome warehouse using provided instructions.  

> Note: Customers are responsible for return shipping unless the product is defective.

---

## 4. Inspection & Approval
- Returned items are inspected within **5 business days**.  
- Approval is based on product condition and policy compliance.  
- Customers are notified via email upon approval or rejection.

---

## 5. Exchanges
- If product is eligible for exchange, a replacement is shipped **within 3 business days** after inspection.  
- Shipping fees may apply for exchanged items depending on circumstances.

---

## 6. Exceptions
- Products damaged by the customer are **not eligible**.  
- Customized or personalized products are **non-returnable**.
