## 1. Overview
NeuraHome Systems IoT devices collect data to provide **smart home automation, analytics, and improved user experience**.  
This policy explains what data is collected, how it is stored, and how it is used.

---

## 2. Types of Data Collected
### a) Device Telemetry
- Device status (on/off, settings, battery level)  
- Sensor readings (temperature, motion, door status)  
- Connectivity logs (Wi-Fi strength, uptime)  

### b) User Interaction Data
- App interactions (device control, automation rules)  
- Commands via voice assistants  
- Notifications received / dismissed  

### c) Event Logs
- Security events (door unlocks, motion detected, alerts)  
- System errors and firmware updates  
- Automation trigger history  

---

## 3. How Data is Collected
- Directly from IoT devices over secure channels  
- Via mobile app interactions  
- Cloud servers aggregate and store telemetry data  

---

## 4. Purpose of Data Collection
- Ensure proper device functioning and uptime  
- Provide analytics for smart automation suggestions  
- Detect and troubleshoot errors remotely  
- Enable notifications and alerts for users  
- Improve AI models for motion/person detection  

---

## 5. Data Storage & Security
- All data encrypted with **AES-256**  
- Cloud storage in secure, certified data centers  
- Access restricted to authorized personnel  
- Retention period:  
  - Event logs: 90 days  
  - User preferences and account info: until account deletion  

---

## 6. User Control & Privacy
- Users can **opt-out** of non-essential data collection in app settings  
- Users can request **export or deletion** of collected data  
- Only anonymized data used for analytics or AI improvements  

---

## 7. Sharing Data
- Data shared with third-party services **only with consent** (e.g., Alexa, Google Home)  
- Law enforcement requests processed according to law  
- Aggregated anonymized data may be used for research or product improvement  

---

## 8. Compliance
- GDPR, CCPA, and other relevant privacy laws  
- Users have the right to access, correct, and delete personal data
