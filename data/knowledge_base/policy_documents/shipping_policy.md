# NeuraHome Systems — Shipping Policy

## 1. Overview
NeuraHome Systems offers reliable shipping services to deliver your smart home devices promptly and safely.  

---

## 2. Shipping Areas
- We ship to **all domestic locations within the USA**.  
- International shipping available to select countries (check website for eligibility).  

---

## 3. Shipping Methods
| Method | Delivery Time | Cost |
|--------|---------------|------|
| Standard | 3–7 business days | Free for orders $50+ |
| Expedited | 1–3 business days | $15 flat rate |
| Express / Same-Day | Same day (select cities) | $25 flat rate |

> Delivery times are estimates and may vary due to weather, carrier delays, or holidays.

---

## 4. Order Processing
- Orders are processed within **24 hours** of confirmation.  
- You will receive a **tracking number** once shipped.  
- You can track your order via your NeuraHome account or the carrier’s website.

---

## 5. Shipping Restrictions
- Delivery is only made to the **address provided at checkout**.  
- P.O. Boxes and APO/FPO addresses may not be supported for certain products.  
- Hazardous or restricted items may require special shipping procedures.

---

## 6. Lost or Damaged Shipments
- Inspect all packages upon delivery.  
- Report **lost or damaged items** to support within **48 hours**.  
- NeuraHome will initiate replacement or refund once verified.
