# NeuraHome Systems — Refund Policy

## 1. Overview
NeuraHome Systems provides refunds for eligible products according to our Return Policy.  
Refunds are processed promptly once returned items are inspected and approved.

---

## 2. Eligibility
- Product must be returned within **30 days** of delivery.  
- Must comply with the **Return Policy** (unused, undamaged, original packaging).  
- Refunds do not apply to:
  - Opened consumables (batteries, bulbs, sensors)  
  - Damaged or modified products

---

## 3. Refund Process
1. Submit a **return request** through your NeuraHome account.  
2. Receive **RMA number** and ship product to NeuraHome warehouse.  
3. Returned items are inspected **within 5 business days**.  
4. Once approved, the refund is processed to the **original payment method**.  

> Note: Bank processing may take 3–7 business days.

---

## 4. Refund Amount
- Full refund for eligible items.  
- Partial refund if item is damaged or missing accessories.  
- Shipping fees are **non-refundable** unless product is defective.

---

## 5. Refund for Defective Products
- Products deemed defective are eligible for **full refund including shipping**.  
- Customer support will guide you to return defective items for inspection.

---

## 6. Refund Denial
Refund may be denied if:
- Item is returned after **30 days**.  
- Item is damaged due to misuse.  
- Product is not in original packaging.
