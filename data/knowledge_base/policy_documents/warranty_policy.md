# NeuraHome Systems — Warranty Policy

## 1. Overview
NeuraHome Systems provides a **limited hardware warranty** for all products to ensure quality and reliability.  
Warranty coverage applies to defects in material or workmanship under normal use.

---

## 2. Warranty Period
| Product | Warranty Duration |
|---------|-----------------|
| NH-Hub X1 | 1 year |
| NH-Cam Pro 360 | 1 year |
| NH-Lock Secure+ | 2 years |
| NH-Bulb Glow RGB+ | 1 year |
| Accessories (cables, adapters) | 6 months |

---

## 3. What is Covered
- Manufacturing defects  
- Faulty components  
- Malfunction under normal operating conditions

---

## 4. What is Not Covered
- Physical damage, misuse, or accidents  
- Water damage not compliant with product IP rating  
- Unauthorized modifications or repairs  
- Consumables (batteries, bulbs) after normal usage  

---

## 5. Warranty Claims
1. Contact NeuraHome support via email or hotline.  
2. Provide proof of purchase and product details.  
3. Support issues an **RMA (Return Merchandise Authorization) number**.  
4. Return the product following the RMA instructions.  
5. Approved claims result in **repair, replacement, or refund** depending on product and availability.

---

## 6. Warranty Process Timeline
- Inspection: 5 business days  
- Repair / Replacement: 7–10 business days  
- Refund (if applicable): 3–7 business days after approval
