## 1. Issue: Hub not connecting to Wi-Fi
### Causes
- Incorrect Wi-Fi password  
- 5 GHz network selected (Hub supports 2.4 GHz & 5 GHz)  
- Router signal too weak  

### Solutions
1. Verify Wi-Fi password and re-enter in the app.  
2. Ensure correct Wi-Fi band is selected (2.4 GHz recommended for distance).  
3. Move hub closer to router or remove obstacles.  
4. Restart hub and router.  

---

## 2. Issue: Hub shows weak connection / yellow LED
### Causes
- Hub too far from router  
- Interference from other devices (microwave, cordless phone)  

### Solutions
1. Relocate hub to open area.  
2. Reduce interference by moving other electronics.  
3. Use Ethernet connection if possible for stable connectivity.  

---

## 3. Issue: Hub offline in app
### Causes
- Hub disconnected from network  
- Internet outage  
- App outdated  

### Solutions
1. Check hub LED status.  
2. Restart hub and router.  
3. Update NeuraHome app to latest version.  
4. Re-add hub in the app if needed.  

---

## 4. Issue: Devices not responding through Hub
### Solutions
1. Confirm hub is online.  
2. Ensure connected devices are powered on and paired.  
3. Reboot hub and affected devices.  
4. Check firmware updates for hub and devices.
