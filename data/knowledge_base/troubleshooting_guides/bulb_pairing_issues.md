## 1. Issue: Bulb not entering pairing mode
### Causes
- Bulb was already paired to another network  
- Power cycle not performed correctly  

### Solutions
1. Turn bulb **off and on 3 times** to reset.  
2. Ensure wall switch is on during pairing.  
3. Confirm bulb is blinking white before pairing in the app.  

---

## 2. Issue: App cannot detect bulb
### Causes
- Phone not connected to 2.4 GHz Wi-Fi  
- Bluetooth or location services disabled  

### Solutions
1. Connect phone to **same 2.4 GHz network**.  
2. Enable Bluetooth and location services on your phone.  
3. Restart the NeuraHome app and retry pairing.  

---

## 3. Issue: Bulb connects but disconnects frequently
### Causes
- Weak Wi-Fi signal  
- Interference from other devices  

### Solutions
1. Move bulb closer to router.  
2. Reduce the number of devices on the same Wi-Fi network.  
3. Reset bulb and re-add in app if needed.  

---

## 4. Issue: Grouping / Multiple Bulbs not syncing
### Solutions
1. Ensure all bulbs are **on the same Wi-Fi network**.  
2. Refresh device list in app.  
3. Re-add bulbs to the group.  
4. Update bulb firmware to latest version.
