# App Errors & Troubleshooting — NeuraHome App

## 1. Issue: App crashes on launch
### Causes
- Outdated app version  
- Corrupted cache or data  
- Device OS compatibility issues  

### Solutions
1. Update NeuraHome app to latest version.  
2. Clear app cache and storage (Settings → Apps → NeuraHome → Clear Cache/Data).  
3. Restart mobile device.  
4. Reinstall the app if issue persists.  

---

## 2. Issue: Cannot log in
### Causes
- Incorrect credentials  
- Account not verified  
- Server maintenance  

### Solutions
1. Verify username and password.  
2. Reset password via “Forgot Password”.  
3. Ensure email account is verified.  
4. Retry after maintenance window.  

---

## 3. Issue: Devices not appearing in app
### Causes
- Device offline or unpaired  
- Hub offline  
- Network issues  

### Solutions
1. Confirm device is powered on and online.  
2. Check hub status and connectivity.  
3. Refresh device list in app.  
4. Re-pair device if necessary.  

---

## 4. Issue: Notifications not received
### Causes
- Push notifications disabled  
- Do Not Disturb mode active  
- Device offline  

### Solutions
1. Enable push notifications in app settings.  
2. Check phone DND or battery saver mode.  
3. Ensure devices are online and synced.  

---

## 5. Issue: App slow or unresponsive
### Solutions
1. Close other background apps to free memory.  
2. Update app and device firmware to latest versions.  
3. Restart phone and hub.  
4. Reduce number of devices connected to hub if possible.
