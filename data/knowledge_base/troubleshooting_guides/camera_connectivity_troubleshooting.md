# Camera Connectivity Troubleshooting — NH-Cam Pro 360

## 1. Issue: Camera not connecting to Wi-Fi
### Possible Causes
- Wrong Wi-Fi password  
- Using 5 GHz network (camera only supports 2.4 GHz)  
- Router out of range or signal weak  

### Solutions
1. Verify Wi-Fi password and enter again in the app.  
2. Switch to 2.4 GHz network if currently on 5 GHz.  
3. Move camera closer to the router.  
4. Restart the router and camera.  

---

## 2. Issue: Camera offline in the app
### Possible Causes
- Power loss  
- Device disconnected from network  
- App not updated  

### Solutions
1. Check power connection.  
2. Ensure Wi-Fi is stable.  
3. Restart the app.  
4. Update camera firmware via the app.  

---

## 3. Issue: App cannot detect camera during setup
### Solutions
- Make sure phone is on the **same 2.4 GHz network**.  
- Enable Bluetooth and location services.  
- Reset camera by power-cycling 3 times.  
- Retry pairing in the app.  

---

## 4. Issue: Poor video quality or lag
### Solutions
- Check Wi-Fi signal strength (≥2 Mbps upload).  
- Reduce the number of devices on the same network.  
- Use Ethernet adapter if available for stable connection.  
- Close background apps on mobile device.
