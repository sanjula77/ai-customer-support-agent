## 1. Issue: Firmware update fails
### Causes
- Poor Wi-Fi connection  
- Device battery low  
- App or server issue  

### Solutions
1. Ensure device is **powered and fully charged** or connected to mains.  
2. Verify stable **2.4 GHz Wi-Fi** connection.  
3. Retry update in the NeuraHome app.  
4. Restart device and app before attempting update again.  

---

## 2. Issue: Device LED blinks red during update
### Causes
- Interrupted update  
- Network drop  

### Solutions
1. Power cycle device.  
2. Reconnect to Wi-Fi and retry update.  
3. If issue persists, contact support for manual firmware update instructions.  

---

## 3. Issue: Device becomes unresponsive after update
### Solutions
1. Perform a **hard reset** of the device.  
2. Re-pair device in the app.  
3. Ensure the app and hub firmware are compatible with the device firmware.  

---

## 4. Preventive Measures
- Always update firmware **overnight** to avoid interruptions.  
- Keep devices close to Wi-Fi router during update.  
- Avoid power interruption while update is in progress.
