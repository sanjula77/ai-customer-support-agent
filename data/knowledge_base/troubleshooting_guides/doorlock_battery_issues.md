## 1. Issue: Low Battery Warning
### Causes
- Batteries are nearing depletion  
- Using low-quality or non-rechargeable batteries  

### Solutions
1. Replace with **4 new AA alkaline batteries**.  
2. Use high-quality, branded batteries for longer life.  
3. Check that batteries are correctly oriented in the compartment.  

---

## 2. Issue: Lock not responding
### Causes
- Batteries are dead  
- Loose battery contacts  

### Solutions
1. Remove battery pack and reinsert correctly.  
2. Clean contacts with a dry cloth to remove corrosion.  
3. Replace batteries if power still does not work.  

---

## 3. Issue: Frequent Low Battery Notifications
### Causes
- High frequency of unlocks/usage  
- Extreme temperatures affecting battery life  

### Solutions
1. Replace with **high-capacity AA batteries**.  
2. Avoid exposure to extreme cold or heat.  
3. Enable energy-saving mode in the app if supported.  

---

## 4. Issue: Lock fails to operate after battery replacement
### Solutions
1. Ensure **battery pack is fully seated** and screws are tightened.  
2. Perform **system reset** via interior module.  
3. Pair the lock again in the NeuraHome App.
